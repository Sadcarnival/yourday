Privacy Policy — your day

Last updated: June 2026

your day is a personal to-do list app. This page explains what data the app collects, why, and how it's used.

What we collect

  • Google account info — when you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture from Google to identify your account.
  • Your task data — the categories, tasks, notes, and settings you create inside the app.
  • App preferences — your selected theme and display name.

How we use it

  • To sync your tasks across the devices you sign into.
  • To personalize the app with your name and chosen theme.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties.
  • We do not use your data for advertising.

Where it's stored

Your data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore and Firebase Authentication), Google's cloud infrastructure. Data is associated only with your signed-in account and is not accessible to other users.

Data retention & deletion

Your data is retained as long as your account exists. You can delete your task data at any time from within the app. To request full account deletion, contact us using the details below and we will remove your data from our systems.

Third-party services

This app uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policy:

  • Google Sign-In
  • Google Firebase

Children's privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a revised date at the top.
